Read Carefully!

AI, or Artificial Intelligence, is the field of computer science focused on creating systems capable of human-like intelligence. Coined in 1956, Alan Turing, often considered the "father of AI," laid the foundation for the field with his groundbreaking work. Machine learning, a crucial aspect of AI, involves developing algorithms that enable systems to learn from data and make decisions without explicit programming. This transformative technology finds applications in various domains, making AI an integral part of our technological landscape.
